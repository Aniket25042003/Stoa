# ICP Profile v3 — Mid-Market B2B SaaS

## Summary

Best-fit customers are VP Marketing-led B2B SaaS teams (50–500 employees) with HubSpot, Gong, and GA4 already in stack. They struggle to connect campaign activity to closed-won revenue and lose weeks to manual reporting.

## Top segments

1. **FinTech SaaS** — Highest ACV ($62k avg), webinar + partner sourced, long cycles but strong expansion
2. **MarTech** — Competitive displacement opportunities vs InsightLoop
3. **HR Tech** — High lead volume, lower win rate; needs alignment tooling

## Top pain points

- Manual campaign reporting (frequency: high)
- Sales-marketing lead quality disputes (high)
- Competitive surprise pricing moves (medium)

## Top objections

- "We already have HubSpot dashboards"
- Implementation time before board meetings
- Price vs InsightLoop after discount

## Buying triggers

- New CMO or VP Marketing hire
- Missed quarterly pipeline target
- Board request for attribution proof

## Win/loss themes

- **Win:** Faster time-to-insight, Gong + HubSpot evidence, cited agent answers
- **Loss:** Budget freeze, InsightLoop price cut, stalled procurement
