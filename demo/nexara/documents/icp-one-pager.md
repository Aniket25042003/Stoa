# ICP One-Pager — Stoa Analytics (2026)

## Ideal customer profile

**Company:** B2B SaaS, 50–500 employees, Series A to C
**Buyer:** VP Marketing, Head of Growth, CMO
**Geography:** North America, UK (primary)

## Firmographics

- Uses HubSpot or Salesforce CRM
- Runs paid + organic campaigns with GA4 or PostHog
- Has sales call recording (Gong preferred)
- 3+ person marketing team

## Pain themes

1. Campaign reporting takes days, not hours
2. Sales disputes marketing lead quality
3. Competitive moves discovered too late
4. Launch orchestration spans 6+ channels without shared evidence

## Best customers (evidence)

- **Acme FinTech** — $85k expansion, webinar-sourced, FinTech
- **CloudLedger** — partner-sourced, fast cycle
- **AdPulse** — paid social convert with strong enablement

## Disqualifiers

- No CRM
- Pure services business without product-led motion
- <20 employees without marketing ops capacity

## Messaging pillars

- "Prove what drives pipeline"
- "One agent, all your GTM evidence"
- "Precomputed intelligence, not generic AI"
