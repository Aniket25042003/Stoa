# AE Loss Analysis — Q4 2025

**Author:** Sales Leadership | **Date:** 2026-01-05

## Executive summary

We lost 8 deals totaling $312k ARR. Primary patterns: budget freezes in FinTech (PayStream), competitive losses to InsightLoop (RevOps Labs), and stalled negotiations in HR Tech.

## Deal post-mortems

**PayStream ($18k)** — Lost to budget. Champion left company mid-cycle. Marketing sourced via retargeting-fall; low intent signal.

**RevOps Labs ($36k)** — Lost to InsightLoop on price after their 20% Pro tier cut. We lacked battlecard refresh on pricing.

**BrightHR ($28k pilot)** — Still open but stalled in Proposal 21 days. Friction: sales wants more proof webinar leads convert.

## Recommendations

1. Refresh InsightLoop battlecard with new pricing
2. Marketing to produce webinar ROI evidence for sales enablement
3. Tighten paid social qualification criteria
